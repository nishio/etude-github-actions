Psychological safety and security
Q: What is the difference between [psychological safety] and [sense of security]?
A: 
	You can think of it as a sense of security.
	And what kind of "sense of security" is that?
	For example
		No one will think you're stupid for asking questions.
		You can't be offended if you have a different opinion than others.
	When individual team members have that sense of security, "there is psychological safety in the team.
		Psychological safety is an attribute of the team, not the individual.
	When teams have psychological safety, it encourages learning within the team and ultimately enhances team outcomes
