From Multidimensional to Tree
[multidimensional], [tree], [one-dimensional].
Converting multidimensional objects into a tree can be done with [Hierarchical Clustering].

It can be converted to one dimensional with a one dimensional [self-organizing map], or even hierarchical clustering with a little ingenuity.

pseudo code
	Merging close data points
		At this time, points with a rank of 2 are removed from the annexation target.

Changing the rank limit from 2 to N results in a tree with at most N-1 children.
