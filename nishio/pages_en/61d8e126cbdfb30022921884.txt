Experiential Process and Meaning Creation of the Kozane Method
Describe the process of KJ method and Kozane method with the concept of "[experience process and meaning creation]".

There are [internal exploration] and [external exploration] (Jiro Kawakita's term)

Internal exploration is the process of exploring one's inner self and creating verbalized fragments

External exploration is the process of exploring outside of yourself and creating verbalized fragments
　For example, interviewing multiple people on the same topic.
　Nishio considers reading books a form of external exploration.

[https://gyazo.com/93a27fe1f6bf12914dfc2a25610bc125]

In neither case are the fragments created in an organized order.
