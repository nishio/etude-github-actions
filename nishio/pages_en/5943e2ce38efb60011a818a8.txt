EPSP
Excitatory postsynaptic potential, the potential at the receiving end of an excitatory synapse. Inhibitory ones are IPSPs because they are Inhibitory.
[https://gyazo.com/ed8a9fb7684e2bc05aa48bd88d55590f] https://7e.biopsychology.com/vs/vs03/vs03.html
A single pulse does not reach the activity threshold of the receiving neuron. If a number of pulses come at short intervals, the threshold is exceeded and firing occurs.
