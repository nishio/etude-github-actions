To belittle others is to belittle oneself
Generalization of the following two stories
　Middle-aged managers who say that "new hires these days are not good" are unintentionally stating that their company is disliked and avoided by the "hot young people" these days.
　People who say "Twitter is full of idiots" are expressing their inability to find and follow people who are not idiots.
