Why do they make fun of us?
Q: Why are they [idiots]?
A: Because he's actually an idiot.
　When they get feedback that they are saying something stupid, they don't think, "I said something stupid, I need to update my knowledge," but instead they think, "I'm making fun of you for no good reason."
　　The former idiots may cease to be idiots in the future, but not the latter.
