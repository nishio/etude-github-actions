[Family Metaphor

When we say [family-oriented management], what we imagine differs from person to person. Because different people have different experiences of family.

	It's not right that "the grandchildren have to put up with a lot of food so grandpa can eat a lot."
	It's funny that "when an adult child wants to leave home and try something, you say, 'Don't think you can go anywhere but here and expect it to work.'"
		Sends the message that you are willing to send them off and that they can always come back if they stumble.
