Crunchy and Mossy
When creating a new UI, whether the UI is "smooth" or "crisp" may have a greater impact on the user than the idea of the UI itself.

The technology to make crispy crispy is unobtrusive but important.
However, the user appreciates that 500msec becomes 50msec, but not 5msec.
As the performance of the machine itself increases, the crispness will eventually produce no user value.

Crunchy is [a natural quality].
