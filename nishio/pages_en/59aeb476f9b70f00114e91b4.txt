	market leader
 	No. 1 in sales
  Cost Leadership Strategy
  [Strategy to maintain market share by leveraging economies of scale to maintain the lowest cost in the industry

	market challenger
 	2nd to 3rd place in sales
  differentiation strategy
  Strategy to capture market share by adding value through differentiation

 market nicher
