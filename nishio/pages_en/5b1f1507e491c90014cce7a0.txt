Feeling of Knowing in Memory and Problem Solving

A study showing that there is a correlation between the feeling of "I'm almost there" and the probability of actually solving the problem later, for memory problems, but not for insight problems that require Aha-like solutions.

Positively expressed, "Even if you think you are not going to solve it, you may suddenly get an inspiration." Negatively expressed, "Even if you feel like you are about to get an inspiration, it may not really be so.

Note that the "probability of solution" was overestimated for both memory and insight questions. Negative~.
