Securitization of Invention
When Watt invented a new [steam engine] and started building a prototype and ran out of money, Roebuck took over his debt and financed the development of the prototype. The price was 2/3 of Watt's royalties when he patented his invention in 1767.
[Invisible Edge] [Amazon https://amzn.to/2FD7ZQH]

There could be an agreement to receive some share of the intellectual property of an invention in exchange for support for an invention that has not been completed, or an agreement to receive a portion of the share in exchange for helping to register a patent on it after the invention is completed. The [securitization] of the invention.
