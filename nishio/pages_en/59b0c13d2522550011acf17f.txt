reproduction on an enlarged or expanded scale
Investing surplus value in the expansion of production.

	>Extended reproduction means, in [Marxian economics], the expansion of the scale of production and reproduction by converting and accumulating some or all of [surplus value] into capital without expending it for consumption by the capitalist.
