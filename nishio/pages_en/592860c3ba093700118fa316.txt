symmetry (physics)
[https://gyazo.com/028921d32b78a30d9526c95b12aa1c44]
The hypothesis that, for a given axis, if there is something on one side of the axis, there must be something corresponding to it on the other side of the axis. See [place of nothingness].
On the right are mirror isomers. The objects in the mirror can be seemingly similar but cannot be superimposed by rotation.
