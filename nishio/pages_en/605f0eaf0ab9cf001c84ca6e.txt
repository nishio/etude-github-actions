[Excerpts from "Opportunities for Decision-Making are Numerous

	[The opportunity for a good decision is not known in advance].
	　>You never know the extent to which a given decision will affect your future at the time you make it.
	　>"The opportunity [to make a decision] that will have a great impact on my life will come in an easy-to-understand way" is as dubious an idea as "Someday a prince who will make me happy will come riding by on a white horse."
	[Snowball of Influence]
　　>Small decisions provide opportunities for other decisions.
　　>[By repeating [the decision of a string of beads], the influence of the decision gradually grows.
