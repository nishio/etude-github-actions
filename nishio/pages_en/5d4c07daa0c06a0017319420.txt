Effect of double-speed playback
A paper that tried 1x, 1.4x, and 1.8x in three different ways and found no significant difference in comprehension, but satisfaction was significantly highest at 1.4x.
[The effects of time-compressed audio and verbal redundancy on learner performance and satisfaction - ScienceDirect https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0747563208000423]
