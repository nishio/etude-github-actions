　　　There are two creative processes of structure that are ultimately expressed as "A and B are similar".
　　　A symbol A is born, a symbol B is born, and then the story is expressed as "there is a similarity L between the two".
　　　A story in which a symbol L is created, and A is represented as being related to L, and B is represented as being related to L. As a result, we can see that A and B are similar in the sense that they are related to L.
