spiral staircase
[https://gyazo.com/4c3549958b130ed63922c6c3e68f9fb1]

A spiral staircase, when viewed from below, is a "circular motion [that] goes around the same place," and when viewed from the side, it is "an oscillating upward motion between [the extremes]."

Drucker: "[History circulates, but the content is of a higher order]."
