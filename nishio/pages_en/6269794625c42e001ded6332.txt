3NOT problem
There is a black box with three inputs [$ x,y,z] and three outputs [$ x',y',z']. The relation between the inputs and outputs is
 [$ x'=\neg x]
 [$ y'=\neg  y]
 [$ z'= \neg z]
is the black box. In the black box, and and or are used as much as you like, but we know that there are only two nots. What is the inside of the black box?
