If being right is not beneficial, which is the better choice?
Suppose there is a correct but unhelpful alternative A and a beneficial but incorrect alternative B
 Q1: Which is the right choice?
 　Of course, A
 Q2: Which is the right choice?
 　Of course, B
 Q3: Which should I choose?
 　So the answer to this question depends on how you interpret the word "good."
