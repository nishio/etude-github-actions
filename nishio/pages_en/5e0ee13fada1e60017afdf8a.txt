Value of Ideas
　The [value] of an [idea] is part of the value that results from the [implementation] of that idea.
　So [ideas that are not implemented have no value].
　For an idea to be implemented, one of the following is required
　　I put out [resources] for execution.
　　Inspire others to put forth the resources to execute.
　Ideas that don't get it done have no value.

Resources here are anything of value needed to execute the idea, such as time, money, knowledge, etc.
