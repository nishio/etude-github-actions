Regional differences in the meaning of innovation
A survey conducted in Japan and India asked the question, "What is [innovation]?" in Japan, 22.5% thought it was about technology, while 25.8% in India thought it was about value-added creation.

As for how to create strengths, while R&D and flexible organizational systems were associated with Japan, in India it was associated with strengthening [individual creativity].

Also, for SURIVIVE, it is considered important to have [technical strengths] in Japan, whereas in India, training in the daily [PDCA] cycle is considered necessary.

Manabu Sawaguchi (2018) "A study on the possibility of international collaboration from the perspective of manufacturing in Japan and India." Proc. of ISPIM Connects Fukuoka
