work style
Essay by [Konosuke Matsushita]. Included in [Opening the Way], p.146.
It was picked up and published in 1968 from a series of short articles that were serialized in the official newspaper of the PHP Research Institute.

summary
	Working with [the sweat of one's] forehead is noble, but without wisdom. It is the progress of human life that the sweat of one's forehead decreases.
 Working an hour less and achieving more than ever before is [an advancement in the way we work].
 It requires [ingenuity]. Work with ease and achieve results, and that is where [social prosperity] will come from.
