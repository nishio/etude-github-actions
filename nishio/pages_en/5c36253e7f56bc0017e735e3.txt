I enjoyed the idea of moving from "forced" to "want to" and the concept of "emotional capital. It was like putting the concept of "human-centeredness" into project design.

The human means of communication used to be voice, with an inescapable set of emotional information,
Overwhelming efficiency has led to the replacement of written communication with electronic methods, and in doing so, emotional information has been dropped.
There is a movement happening to reclaim it in the form of [emoji] and stamps, but maybe ultimately we will communicate through graphics.
