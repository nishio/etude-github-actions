Niebuhr's Prayer
Father, give me
	The [courage] to change what must be changed,
	Accepting the inevitable [tranquility],
	And give us the [insight] to discern them.

Common name for an initially untitled prayer attributed to the American theologian [Reinhold Niebuhr] (1892-1971).
	[https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/ニーバーの祈り Niebuhr prayer - Wikipedia]
	Original is a little longer.

[serenity]: [peace], [tranquility], [tranquility of mind].

[* courage] to change what must be altered,
[* serenity] to accept what cannot be helped,
and the [* insight] to know the one from the other.
