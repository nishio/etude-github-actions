	>Caesar's famous saying, "People only see what they want to see," was originally a translation of "The Story of the Romans" in Shiono-san's own words.
 > "[People are] willing to believe what they wish."
 >[Gallic Wars], vol. 3, ch. 18.
	>The original Latin text is Fere libenter homines id quod volunt credunt
 >In the Kodansha Gakujutsu Bunko translation of Ryonosuke Kunihara's account of the Gallic War, the relevant part is "And [the general human tendency to assume that] what is wished for is willingly true."
