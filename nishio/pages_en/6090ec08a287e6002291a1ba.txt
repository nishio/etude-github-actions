 *1316611602*Taxi drivers are paid 40 times more than doctors.
 I'm reposting it here because it's hard to search for it later if I'm writing it down on Twitter.
 >>
 When I read the title, "Doctors and Taxi Drivers Earn Forty Times More in Cuba," I thought that doctors were treated well, but it was the opposite. I thought that doctors were treated well in Cuba, but it turned out to be the opposite. Taxi drivers, who earn foreign currency from tourists, are 40 times more expensive than doctors, who work in the domestic industry.
