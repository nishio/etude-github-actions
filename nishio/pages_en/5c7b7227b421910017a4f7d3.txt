Why-Why Analysis
>Why-Why analysis is a means of verifying the effectiveness of countermeasures to a problem by repeatedly presenting the factors that caused the problem ("why") and then presenting the factors that caused the problem ("[why]"). [It is one of the representative methods that make up the Toyota Production System.
