Work to think about what to do
[teal organization#5ac8b4fdaff09e000090489c]
> [Automated] technology will make machines more efficient than humans in "doing exactly what they are told to do.
> The pressure to "improve efficiency" in achievement-oriented organizations leads to the stripping of that work from human beings.
> Improved information transmission technology has eliminated the need for people to be superiors.
> As a result, all employees will be engaged in the job of "thinking about what to do" that top management used to do.
