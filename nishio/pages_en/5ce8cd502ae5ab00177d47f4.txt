Three types of quality
Three types of [quality].
	[Search quality], [Experience quality], [Trust quality]
	[Search quality]
		Quality that consumers can evaluate before purchasing a product
		For example, clothes can be tried on before purchase.
	[Experience Quality]:.
		Quality that consumers can evaluate after purchasing the product
		For example, restaurant food can be evaluated after eating it.
	Trust Quality]:: [Trust Quality]:: [Trust Quality]: [Trust Quality
		Difficult to evaluate even after purchasing product
		For example, it is difficult to move into more than one nursing home and compare them.
