nishio-a
[/nishio-a]
Experiment with mechanically importing as much of Yasukazu Nishio's[/nishio] content as possible
The "a" in nishio-a stands for all and automatic.

2019/10/29
	83 past lecture slides imported 3964 pages (including this page)
	Based on [/masui]'s Book2Scrapbox, with support for multiple imports of non-scanned PDFs
		https://github.com/nishio/Book2Scrapbox
	The first 12 characters <pdf id> of the PDF md5hash are used as the PDF identifier
		Page title can be `<pdf id>` or `<pdf id>,<page id>`.
		The page with `<pdf id>` does not exist.
