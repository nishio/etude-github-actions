Misconception of parallel work as multiple employment contracts
 Among those who think "[side jobs] and [multiple jobs]" are difficult, we found that a good number of them have an implicit image of "signing employment contracts with two companies.
 Employment contracts are a heavy monetization method, with many restrictions on both the employer and the employee, and it seems that many people are unaware of the fact that they can be done more easily.
 There are many other ways to make money that do not involve employment, such as posting affiliate links on blogs and earning referral fees, selling handicrafts as a hobby on Mercari and earning money for it, writing articles for technical magazines and earning profits from the sale of stocks...so there are many ways to make money that do not involve employment, so I think you should start by thinking about it. I think that's the first thing to think about.
