 *1266864838* Disruptive innovation explained in 3 lines
 Discussed with id:gaso (http://d.hatena.ne.jp/gasso/20100222) and summarized in 3 lines.
 
 >>
 Lower performance products based on the same technology as existing products
 Sometimes innovation comes from bringing something to market.
 We call it disruptive innovation.
 <<
 
 Now you're thinking, "What, innovation?" read the bottom three lines carefully. If you can easily read this, you misunderstand the meaning of the word innovation.
 
 >>
 Lower performance products based on the same technology as existing products
 Putting it on the market can cause phenomenon X.
 Call it phenomenon X-typeD.
 <<
 
 Schumpeter's definition of the word "innovation" does not mean positive technological innovation.
