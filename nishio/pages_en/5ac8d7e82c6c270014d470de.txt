There are two interpretations.
	A: Each customer's utility estimate is a point estimate, and the estimates vary from person to person.
	B: The customer's utility estimate is a distribution, and which point of the distribution is chosen as the representative value varies from person to person.
 Advantages of B
 	Can express the convergence of anxiety due to lack of information as more information becomes available.
 Disadvantages of B
 	There is no reason for the distribution to be shared across people.
