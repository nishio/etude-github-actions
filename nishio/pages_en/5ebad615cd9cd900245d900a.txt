Those who do not seek enhancements are not customers.
　Most software enhances human capabilities in some way #Human Augmentation
　Some humans believe that they do not need to enhance their abilities.
　　Case 1: Thinks he is competent enough without relying on software
　　Case 2: They don't see the value in developing that ability.
　You don't have to convince these people.
　　They are simply not [customers].
　　If you have plenty of resources, it's worthwhile to persuade and convert [customers].
　　but not a subject to focus on at this stage of low resources.
　　Related: [Product/User Fit].
